# Altimet

**A calmer way to plan your day.** Altimet is a neumorphic Android task,
routine, and calendar planner — local-first, private by design, with optional
EU-hosted cloud sync.

- **Tasks & routines** — flexible recurrence, priorities, subtasks, and reminders
- **Calendar & statistics** — activity heatmaps, streaks, and weekly insights
- **Three live-switchable themes** with full dark mode
- **Private by design** — works offline, no ads, no third-party tracking

*Coming soon to Google Play.*
